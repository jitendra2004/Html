Biography of Nikola Tesla

Early life

Nikola Tesla was born on July 10, 1856,in Smiljan,which was part of the Austrian Empirre (modern-day Croatia).
His father was a priest, and his mother was an inventor who encourahged his cursioty from a young age.

Career

Tesla moved to the United States in 1884 to work for Thomas Edison. He later invented the alternating current (AC) electrical sytem, which laid the foundation for modern electricity distribution.
he also conducted pioneering experiments in wirelless communcation and X-rays.

Achievements

Tesla held over 300 patents during his lifetime. He is the best known for his contribution to the development of AC power, the Telsa coil, and wireless technologies .
Despite his many contributions, Tesla faced financial difficultes later in life.

Legacy

Tesla's work has left a lasting impact on modern technology. His inventions have paved the way for many of the electrical sysytems we use today.
He is remembered as a visionary who was ahead of his time.
